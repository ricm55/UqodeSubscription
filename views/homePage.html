    U HACK 2022

    22 Janvier 2022

    Countdown

    U HACK 2022 is UQODE's first innovation competition. The competition will take place over a 24-hour period during which participants will have to develop the best possible product. The theme will be announced at the opening ceremony. More than $13,700 in prizes will be drawn throughout the competition. Teams must be composed of at least one person and at most 4 people. The competition will be held entirely on discord and a dedicated room for team formation will be presented in order to facilitate team formation in case participants would like to join another team. More information will be sent to you by email after your registration.
